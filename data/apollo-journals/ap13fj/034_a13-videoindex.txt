Apollo 13 Onboard 16-mm film footage
Apollo 13 - 16-mm magazine 1135-A
00:33 The second part of the Transposition, Docking and Extraction task with the CSM facing Lunar Module, Aquarius, parked in the top of the S-IVB third stage. The major part of the film shows the top of the LM growing in the field of view as the CSM approaches until the docking target dominates. Soft dock is at 01:57. This video is presented at 24 frames per second, regardless of the original shooting speed. Its image has been flipped to counter the fact that it was shot into a mirror.
