Apollo 14 landing from PDI to Touchdown
An annotated portrayal of the Apollo 14 landing on the Moon with captions to explain the various stages of the descent. This is part of the Apollo Flight Journal, Apollo 14 collection.
Apollo 14 Onboard Videos
Apollo 14 - 16-mm magazine 1215-A
00:13 Lunar Module 'Antares' is seen housed at the top of the Saturn V's S-IVB third stage. Taken from the Command Service Module 'Kitty Hawk' during the Transposition, Docking and Extraction exercise three hours and ten minutes into the flight. Detritus left over from the separation is still floating about. 02:56 The CSM makes its first contact with the LM. However, the capture latches at the tip of the docking probe fail to catch and the CSM rebounds out again. 03:21 Stu Roosa makes a second attempt which also fails. 03:41 Subsequent approach by the CSM on the LM. It wasn't until the sixth attempt that a successful docking was achieved. This video is presented at 24 frames per second, regardless of the original shooting speed.
